Exploring Delhi

Delhi, the capital city of India, is a vibrant metropolis that seamlessly blends history and modernity. With its rich cultural heritage, stunning architecture, and bustling markets, Delhi offers a unique experience to every visitor.


One of the most iconic landmarks in Delhi is the Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that stands as a testament to the city's Mughal past. The fort's impressive red sandstone walls and intricate carvings attract millions of tourists each year.


Another must-visit attraction is India Gate, a war memorial dedicated to Indian soldiers who lost their lives during World War I. The surrounding gardens provide a peaceful escape from the city's hustle and bustle.
